JavaScript Game - Mancala

Instructions

  1. The game starts with Player 1 picking one of the spaces on their side of the board.
  2. Moving counterclockwise, the pebbles are deposited into each following space (excluding the opponent’s score space) until the pebbles run out.
  3. If the last pebble the player deposits is in their score space, they get another turn.
  4. If the last pebble the player deposits is in an empty space, the player captures any pieces in the space directly across the board and places them directly in their score.
  5. This process repeats until one player’s side of the board is empty.
  6. When one player’s half of the board is empty, the other player collects all of the pebbles on their side of the board for their score.
  7. Whoever has the most pieces in their score space wins.
